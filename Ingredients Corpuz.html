Sesame Mango Smoothie

Sesame seeds and cashew butter add a nice nutty flavor to this mango and spinach green smoothie that is a great on-the-go breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1 small cucumber, roughly chopped
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 2 tablespoons cashew butter
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 cup water
