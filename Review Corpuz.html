Game Review

Marvel Future Fight

Gameplay 8/10
The gameplay was good but they need to fix lags and some bugs.
Graphics and Effects 7/10
The Garphics are good but the effects are not very good because its a bit laggy and it is not like in movies.
Characters 10/10
The Characters in every marvel group are included in the game, they are playable and the looks are the same as in movies and comics.
Overall 8/10
The game was really good in time consuming and especially entertainment but it takes a lot of work to recieve rewards.
